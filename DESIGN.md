## Design System: StadiumIQ Light

### Pattern
- **Name:** Light Enterprise Dashboard
- **Conversion Focus:** Operational clarity. White background, high legibility.
- **Color Strategy:** Clean white backgrounds, subtle grey borders, bright blue accents, clear status colors (green/orange/red).
- **Sections:** Header, Sidebar, Dashboard Content (Cards, Tables, Maps).

### Style
- **Name:** Pure Light Mode
- **Mode Support:** Light ✓ Only | Dark ✗ No
- **Keywords:** Light theme, white background, corporate, enterprise, clean, minimal, bright, high contrast text on white.
- **Best For:** Enterprise operations, dashboards, day-time monitoring.
